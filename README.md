# Design Opportunities

Curated opportunities for product design, UX/UI, UX research, design systems, and adjacent early-career creative technology roles.

This repo is modeled after public opportunity lists, but tuned for the kind of design path I want to track: internships, apprenticeships, fellowships, job boards, and portfolio resources that can help turn a design portfolio into real applications.

## Jason's Shortlist

Best-fit targets to check first when time is tight:

1. Product design and UX internships at major product companies.
2. Apprenticeships that produce portfolio-ready UX case studies.
3. Remote-friendly design internships through Handshake and AIGA.
4. Fellowships that connect design with community, education, or public-interest work.

## How To Use This

- Check `High` priority links first.
- Treat `Monitor` as a company or program page that may post design roles seasonally.
- Treat `Job Board` as a recurring search source rather than a direct listing.
- Move closed roles into `ARCHIVE.md` instead of deleting them, so future cycles are easier to spot.
- Add recent internship rows in `data/internships.csv`.
- Add official company boards in `data/company_sources.csv` so the daily refresh can scan them.
- Add broader resources in `data/opportunities.csv`.
- Run `python3 scripts/generate_readme.py` after editing either CSV.

## Good Fit Criteria

Prioritize opportunities that match at least two of these:

- Product design, UI/UX, UX research, design systems, prototyping, design engineering, or visual/product storytelling.
- Student, intern, apprentice, fellow, early-career, or no-heavy-experience requirement.
- Strong portfolio output: shipped product work, case study material, research artifacts, or real client/team collaboration.
- Remote, hybrid, California, Arizona, or major design hub availability.
- Clear application path, deadline, or recurring cycle.
